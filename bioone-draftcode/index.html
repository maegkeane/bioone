Our Mission in Action

BioOne was founded in 1999 by both library and publisher interests to address the inequities posed by commercial journal publishing. Today, BioOne remains committed to meeting library, publisher, and researcher needs through a growing portfolio of products.

Our Publications

BioOne Complete

BioOne Complete is an aggregation of more than 190 subscribed and open-access titles in the biological, ecological, and environmental sciences.

With BioOne Complete, we keep costs low for libraries while strengthening small to mid-size independent society publishers. BioOne Complete's unique business model supports reinvests precious library funds in the scholarly enterprise, with over $35M returned to BioOne publishers to date.

Elementa: Science of the Anthropocene

Elementa: Science of the Anthropocene is an open-access journal published on a public-good basis. Elementa’s fundamental role is to support an interdisciplinary community of practice to address the Earth’s largest challenges, such as climate change.
